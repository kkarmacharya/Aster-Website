Aster Innovations

Aster Innovation, engages in quality products and services in web-page design, software design, mobile apps, cloud services and other varieties of technology related services within. It operates through following geographical segments: Nepal, Sri Lanka, Australia and USA. Nepal is the headquarter for Aster Innovation while operating other countries for different technical supports and various international projects.

Our Technical and Business Domain Specialist teams are from Nepal, Sri Lanka, and the USA.AST will always follow the technology industry quality standards and methodologies in designing, developing, implementing, and supporting your customized software product in Nepal. Aster also undertakes digital animation software product development for the Mass Media Industry as well as eGovernment, financial ERP system, role-based Management Team’s Dashboards, Mobile Apps, Web App, Web Portals, and integration those with the Artificial Intelligence (AI), Internet of Things (IoT), Business Intelligence Tools (BI), and AR/VR systems.
